Connect your OSAIO cameras to Homey. Multiple brands use OSAIO, incluing GNCC, WOLFANG, Surfola and more. If your device is not yet supported, please request support in the forum topic. Note that only 1 device can be logged in at a time. This means that when you log in to the Homey app, your phone app will automatically log out. To prevent this, you can create a second OSAIO account and let Homey use that account instead. Share the camera to the second account using the sharing feature in the OSAIO mobile app, and then you can use the phone app and Homey at the same time. 2FA is not yet supported, so make sure that's disabled for your second account.
