Thank you for installing the OSAIO app! Some setup is required for this app to function properly.


The OSAIO mobile app only allows one active session per account. This means that when Homey logs in to your OSAIO account, your phone app will log out. Therefore, it is recommended to create a second OSAIO account for Homey and share your cameras with that account. This way, you can keep using both the OSAIO mobile app and Homey at the same time.


If any camera model that you use is missing, or your camera isn't found, please let me know in the forum topic!
